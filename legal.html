We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Information: When you visit our site, we may collect non-personal data such as your browser type, device information, and pages visited.
  • Resume Content: If you create or upload content on ResumePro, it remains private unless you choose to share or download it.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve website performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Improve and personalize the ResumePro experience.
  • Monitor website traffic and performance.
  • Ensure website security and prevent misuse.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to analyze site usage and enhance user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

5. Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your information. However, no website is completely secure, so we advise users to avoid sharing sensitive personal details.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not sell or rent your data. However, we may use third-party tools (such as Google Analytics) to track website performance.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request data deletion.
  • Opt-out of cookie tracking.
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@resumepro.com.

About Us

We are two childhood friends who turned our passion for technology and problem-solving into something meaningful. One of us is the visionary, always coming up with innovative ways to simplify life, while the other is the builder, a sharp-eyed critic who transforms those ideas into functional, polished products through code.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to help as many users as we can by creating intuitive, accessible, and effective tools that solve real-world problems. We believe that technology should make life easier, not more complicated. With ResumePro, we aim to remove the frustration from resume building by offering a seamless, hassle-free experience. Driven by innovation and a commitment to user success, we combine big ideas with sharp execution, ensuring that every feature we build is practical, efficient, and designed to help users move forward in their careers.

Why We Started

We know how frustrating and time-consuming it can be to create the perfect resume. Many people struggle with formatting, wording, or even figuring out what to include. On top of that, Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) can be tricky to navigate, often filtering out resumes before they even reach a recruiter. That’s why we built ResumePro—a solution designed to take the stress out of the process. Whether it's crafting the right content, optimizing for ATS, or ensuring a polished format, our goal is to help you create a resume that gets noticed.

Our Promise

We're committed to making ResumePro better every day. Whether it's improving features, listening to user feedback, or adding new functionalities, we're here to make sure you get the best experience possible. And the best part? It's completely free. No hidden costs, no subscriptions—just a powerful, easy-to-use tool designed to help you succeed.
